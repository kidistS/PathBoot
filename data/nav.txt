Parental leave requires 6 months work.
Apply via NAV portal.
Employer confirmation needed.

Parental leave can be taken by either parent, but only one parent can take it at a time.
The leave can be taken in full or in parts, but must be taken within 3 years of the child's birth.
During parental leave, you will receive a percentage of your salary, depending on your income and the length of the leave.
You can also choose to take unpaid parental leave, but this will not provide any financial support.
It's important to plan your parental leave in advance and communicate with your employer to ensure a smooth transition and to understand your rights and responsibilities during this time.
